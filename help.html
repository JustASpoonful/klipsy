How to use Klipsy.

Follow these simple steps to get help with your work in seconds.

YOUR HOMEWORK PAGE
Start Here

01. Open the "Mini Window"

Before you do anything else, look for the Square Icon in the top right corner of the Klipsy header.

This pops Klipsy into a floating window. You can move it around and it will stay visible even when you click on your homework.

Step Two

02. "Show" Klipsy your page

Inside your floating window, click the big button that says "Attach Source." A list of your tabs will appear.

How to choose:

Pick the tab where your question is. Once you click "Share," Klipsy is officially connected and ready to look at your screen!

Browser Tab
Final Step

03. Hit "Scan Page"

Whenever you're stuck on a question, just click Scan Page. The glowing Orb will start "thinking."

A

Klipsy takes a picture of what you see.

B

The answer appears instantly inside the Orb.

The "Too Long; Didn't Read" Version:

1

Click the Square Icon to pop out the window.

2

Connect it to your homework tab.

3

Click Scan Page and let the Orb solve it.
